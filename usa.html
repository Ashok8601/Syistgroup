Who We Are

SyistGroup is a developer-focused educational initiative by Ashok Kumar, a B.Tech CSE student at HMFA Memorial Institute of Technology under AKTU. With practical experience in Flask, Django, Python, SQLite3, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, and modern web frameworks, Ashok has created several impactful real-world projects like:

  • Rojgar Setu – a job connectivity platform for laborers and employers in India
  • Acart – a simple but powerful e-commerce site built using Flask and SQLite3
  • Resume Maker – an AI-powered tool to generate resumes in multiple templates
  • WhatsApp Web Clone – real-time chat web app with profile, media, and status support
